SIGNS AND WARNINGS

Best Practices

Often, one can secure their home without paying anything. Instead, simply being more aware and cautious is all you need to do. For starters, intruders shy away from homes that look occupied. One tip is to keep lights and noise inside the house while you're away. What makes this particular solution better is if you have a pet dog. While it does not have to be a guard dog, having one is a deterrent. Using signs also is beneficial. The signs will notify potential intruders that you own dogs, you have cameras, and you're armed. All of these types of signs can be plastered right at the front of your house. With this, you'd decrease the likelihood of your home being a target. Simply limiting who has access to your home also is a valuable tip when trying to keep it secure.
